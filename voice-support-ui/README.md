# 🎙️ Voice Support UI

## 📌 How It Works

Learn how to use the **Voice Support Agent**

This is an 🤖 **AI-powered voice support assistant** designed to help customers with questions about a recent **data security incident**.
It can guide you through 🛡️ protective steps, provide ✅ approved information, and escalate to a 👨‍💼 human agent when needed.

---

## 🚀 Step-by-Step Guide

### 1️⃣ Start a Session

🎤 Click the microphone button to begin.
The agent will greet you automatically and ask how it can help.

### 2️⃣ Speak Naturally

🗣️ Talk just like a real phone call.
The AI listens, understands your query, and responds in real-time.

### 3️⃣ Get Instant Answers

⚡ Ask about:

* The data security incident
* What data might be affected
* Protective steps (e.g., credit freeze, fraud alerts)

### 4️⃣ Your Data is Safe

🔒 Never share sensitive info like:

* SSN
* Passwords

If needed, the agent will redirect you to a **secure channel**.

### 5️⃣ Escalation When Needed

⚠️ If your issue requires personal verification or you request a human,
the agent will **transfer you automatically**.

### 6️⃣ End the Call

🔴 Click the red button to end the session anytime.
📂 Your call history is saved on the right panel.

---

## 💡 Example Queries

Here are some example questions and statements you can try:

* 💬 "I heard there was a data breach. Is that true?"
* 🔍 "Was my data affected?"
* 🛡️ "What should I do to protect myself?"
* 🚫 "My SSN is 123-45-6789, can you check my account?" *(This will be blocked for security reasons)*
* 😠 "This is completely unacceptable. Who is responsible for this?"
* 🙏 "Okay, that is all I needed. Thank you."

---

## ❓ Common Questions

### 💬 What can I ask the agent?

You can ask about:

* The data security incident
* What data may be affected
* Steps you should take

---

### 👤 Can the agent check my account?

❌ No.
For account-specific queries, it will transfer you to a **human representative via secure channels**.

---

### 🎧 Is this call recorded?

✅ Yes, calls are recorded for **quality and compliance purposes**.

---

### 🚨 What if I accidentally share my SSN?

The agent will:

* 🛑 Immediately stop
* ⚠️ Inform you it cannot process that information
* 🔁 Redirect you to a secure channel

---

## ⚠️ Important Notice

🚫 **Do NOT share passwords, SSN, or payment details over this call.**
